Anki Decks for Genki

Here you can download Anki decks for Genki 3rd Edition, based on the vocabulary/kanji found on Genki Study Resources and in the Genki textbooks. Click any of the links in the sections below to download a deck.


Quick Navigation

If you would like to download all of the decks at once, click here to download the repository. Once downloaded, unzip the contents, then navigate to resources/tools/decks/lessons or resources/tools/kanji/lessons to access the decks.

You can also find xlsx vocabulary lists for Genki here!

How to Import the Decks

Once you have your decks, open Anki and click "Import File" (or go to File > Import...) to import your decks.

That's it, you're done. Now you're ready to study the vocab!
